Urban Exploration

/ Northwestern

+ ActionScript 3, XML, Illustration (Adobe Illustrator CS5)

Ha. Good ol' Flash. But this was my first foray into coding in general, so it's worth showing here nonetheless. This is a graphic coded to show interesting locations for urban explorers to visit, including info about location, dangers, ease of access, preparation tips, and photos.

Urban exploration is the exploration of man-made structures, usually abandoned ruins or unseen components of man-made environments. Photography and historical interest/documentation are heavily featured in the hobby.
