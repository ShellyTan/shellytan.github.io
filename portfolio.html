"The Mad Barista" was my first time building a full-scale website. (Forgive any jankiness, especially with the responsive design.) A group project for Jeremy Gilbert and Emily Withrow's "Unclass" of Spring 2013, this site focused on the science behind brewing the perfect cup of coffee.
